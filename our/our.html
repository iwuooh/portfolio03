OUR CUPS RUNNETH OVER WITH THE JUICE OF REAL TASMANIAN FRUITS: APPLES AND CHERRIES AND BERRIES AND MORE. OUR HANDCRAFTED TASMANIAN PREMIUM CIDERS REALLY CELEBRATE THE SEASONS.

APPLE CIDER

4.5% ALC/VOL TASTING NOTES:

She’ll be apples! Four different kinds, in fact, are blended into our pure, elegantly refreshing, thirst-quenching apple cider — granny smith, gala, golden delicious and jonathon. A great all-rounder. A sparkling apple cider with beautiful clarity and champagne-like bubbles. Fresh apple aromas with an effervescent apple tang and a lingering medium-dry finish. Serve well chilled.

PEAR CIDER

4.5% ALC/VOL TASTING NOTES:

This enchanting pear and apple blend is soft and alluring: not too sweet, not too dry. The pear cider component is given extra love by being part fermented in chardonnay oak barrels; an apple infusion keeps it fresh and clean. An enticing pear and apple cider blend (beurre bosc and packham pears and a dash of apples). Spicy pear flesh and skin on the nose, with subtle oak notes. Rich pear flavours given extra mouthfeel by partial barrel ageing, nicely balanced by a crunchy apple zip. Lovely on its own, or paired with spicy Asian cuisines.

CHERRY CIDER

8% ALC/VOL TASTING NOTES:

Uniquely Tasmanian, our Cerise (French for ‘cherry’) is a purely Pagan innovation: a bewitching blend of rich dark cherry juice (from plump cherries grown in the orchard beside the cellar door) and dry apple cider. A lush blend of cherry juice and dry apple cider, with a spicy perfumed nose and claret/burgundy hues. The palate hums with dark cherries and plums, with rich Christmas fruit cake notes. Great with crumbly cheddar.

STRAWBERRY

8% ALC/VOL

Not sweet at all. A blend of Cygnet strawberries and dry apple cider, with a tingly spine that finishes bone dry. Great with bitter dark chocolate.

BLUEBERRY

8% ALC/VOL

Simply exquisite, this blushing beauty is blended from plump Tassie blueberries and dry apple cider, with exquisite notes of blueberry, violet and rose petal.

QUINCE

8% ALC/VOL

An ambrosial, savoury blend of dry apple cider and fragrant quince. A celebratory tipple, begging for a champagne flute, and perhaps a cheese platter.

Winning Best in Class at the 2016 Australian Cider Awards, and 100% barrel aged, this wonderful beast of a cider is one we’re super proud of.
